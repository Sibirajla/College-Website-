Welcome to BITSATHY

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology is an Autonomous, Self-financing Engineering College, Approved by AICTE, New TamilNadu and Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Nestled on the banks of The River Bhavani, BIT campus provides environment for learning in harmony with nature, away from the odds of the city life. The spacious and the earth hugging buildings punctuated with landscaped courtyards and pathways are designed to emphasise the business ethics and or characteristics of an excellent centre for learning. The Campus hosts well planned academic blocks, computer centres, lecture halls, libraries, laboratories, conference halls, staff quarters, hostels and students' centres. The Campus also houses a co-operative store, ATM (Axis, SBI and KVB) and a clinic to attend to the general health of the students and staff.

Our Facilities

A wide of range of facilities have been provided to the students to ensure a conducive learning atmosphere as well as a pleasant stay in campus.
NSIT ensures that various aspects of student welfare have been well taken care of.

Central Library

Central Library of Bannari Amman Institute Of Technology is functioning from 1st & Mezzanine Floors of Library Building. Library Building is centrally situated.

Sports

BITSATHY has a range of facilities including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, lawn tennis court, volleyball court, and indoor gymnasium. There is also a running/jogging track on the campus.

Food & Canteens

In addition to the regular mess facilities in the hostels, the BITSATHY campus boasts of affordable canteens spread throughout the campus. All canteens provide delicious food at reasonable prices.
